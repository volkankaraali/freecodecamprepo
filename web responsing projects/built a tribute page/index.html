Uğur Şahin

German-Turkish oncologist and immunologist.

Uğur Şahin with his wife Özlem Türeci.

Here's a time line of Uğur Şahin's life:

  • 1965 Born in İskenderun,Hatay,Turkey.

  • 1984 He began to study medicine at the University of Cologne Faculty of Medicine.
  • 1993 He did his PhD at the University of Cologne.
  • 1999 He received habilitation in molecular medicine and immunology.
  • 2006 He received the title of professor at the University of Mainz.
  • 2006 He married with Özlem Türeci.
  • 2019 He and his team found the corona vaccine.
